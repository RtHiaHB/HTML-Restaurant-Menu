Our Origin Story

H-Thai-ML is an authentic Thai food experience from Mungkot Lam, one of the finest chefs to come from Thailand.

Our story begins in 1994, with our first location on Wither and Vine in Cisco City. We quickly gained a reputation for the quality and authentic flavor of our food. In 1997, we opened our second location in Kamar-taj. Today, we have a grand total of four locations throughout the state.
